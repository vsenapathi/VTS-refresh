Our Pillars of Service

VT Seva's service efforts are concentrated in 4 main areas. Learn more about each below!

Education

School and college for the visually impaired and schools for the underprivileged

Cancer Awareness

Medical camps in rural communities, and walkathons in our local communities

Environmental Protection

Sustainability education initiatives and street clean-ups across the nation

Disaster Relief

On the ground support and resource distribution in afflicted communities

A Healthy Kid is A Happy Kid

Health checkups, nourishing diet, and an emphasis on physical and emotional well-being are all ways VT Seva's schools have enabled children to flourish.

Education for Girls

Many girls in rural areas lack access to educational and extracurricular opportunities. At VT Seva schools, our girls receive a safe environment in which they can grow academically, and personally!

The Power of Education

To many of our students, a good education is the key to breaking generational cycles of poverty. The community as a whole benefits from educating their children and prospers.

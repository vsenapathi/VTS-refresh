About VT Seva

Mission

Our CEO and Founder, His Holiness Sri Chinna Jeeyar Swamiji started Vikasa Tarangini in 1992 to promote understanding and tolerance in society by empowering the youth of India. His goal was to imbibe in children a passion for public service and equip them with the skills to develop and lead philanthropic projects.

Thirty years on, this vision has given birth to VT Seva. A global non-profit service organization established in 2008, VT Seva is driven by over 75,000 active volunteers in the USA, UK, Australia, and Tanzania.

Our mission is to provide education and healthcare to impoverished communities around the world, such that they may live with dignity and pride.

Our Pillars of Service

VT Seva's service efforts are concentrated in 4 main areas, each chosen to address key factors in community well-being.
